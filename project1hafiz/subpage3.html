About

Shikder Hafiz
Shikder Hafiz is a world reknown Bengali chef. His recipes have been published in many cookbooks all over the world. As a retired chef, he is currently pursuing a Computer Science degree at Queensboro College. In his spare time, he likes to cook for family and friends and is an avid fitness enthusiast and martial artist.
